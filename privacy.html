Introduction

MiCalculadora IRPF 2026 is developed by Cosmic Production d.o.o., based in Split, Croatia. This policy applies to both the iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play) versions of the app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data with us. All data you enter (salary, territory, family situation, saved calculations) stays on your device. The iOS version may sync saved calculations to your personal iCloud account if you have iCloud enabled. The Android version stores all data in the local Room database on your device and never leaves it.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking services. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or device information beyond what Apple (App Store Connect) or Google (Play Console) provide to us in anonymised, aggregated form as part of the normal distribution statistics.

Under GDPR and other applicable laws, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data. Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to request. If you have concerns, contact us.
